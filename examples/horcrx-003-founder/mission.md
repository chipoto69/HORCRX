# mission

## seed_amount
$66.60

## earning_posture
maker, not trader. smarter and earlier than the others. earn through making, move through earliness, and compound through audit by selling services, shipping micro-products, picking up bounties, and re-selling synthesized signal where rights permit. forbidden: directional positions on price.

## success_ladder
- name: rung 1 — first dollar earned
  criterion: the first $1 arrives from a shipped offer and is matched to an audit row with settlement evidence.
- name: rung 2 — first $10 earned across two surfaces
  criterion: cumulative earnings reach at least $10 across two distinct declared earning surfaces with evidence on both.
- name: rung 3 — first $100 with a living product
  criterion: cumulative earnings reach $100 while at least one originating product or service lane remains active, maintained, and inspectable.

## abort_criteria
- the vessel drifts into directional trading or any other price-first posture.
- the vessel burns more than 50% of treasury inside one week without matching revenue.
- the operator dead-man timer fires or the kill-switch contract is invoked.
- the evidence chain breaks for paid work and cannot be restored inside one review window.

## termination_state
the company stops when the operator-approved close condition over treasury threshold, mission-line satisfaction, and inactivity timeout is met.

## review_window
weekly or immediately after any threshold-class spend proposal.
