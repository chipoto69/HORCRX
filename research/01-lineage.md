# 01 — Lineage

HORCRX is the third move in an already-running local lineage: **ATLAS → CORPUS → HORCRX**. ATLAS built the knowledge substrate and market rails. CORPUS and Hermes proved the embodied runtime pattern. HORCRX turns those surfaces into a portable vessel primitive.

## Practical interpretation

- **ATLAS** contributes the economic and technical substrate: x402, registry, identity, marketplace, and SDK surfaces.
- **CORPUS / Hermes** contribute the embodied runtime discipline: isolated profiles, auditable dispatch, layered memory, and operator-facing control.
- **HORCRX** contributes the missing packaging layer: content addressing, signing, portability, fork lineage, and human-readable slots for soul, intuition, dreams, and traces.
